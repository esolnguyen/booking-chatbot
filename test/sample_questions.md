# Sample Questions for the Travel Booking Assistant

## Basic Recommendations

1. What are the best flight options from SFO to Tokyo for a business trip?
2. Can you recommend a hotel in Tokyo with a gym?
3. What's the cheapest flight and hotel combo for New York?
4. I need a non-stop flight from SFO to London. What's available?
5. Which hotels in Singapore have a pool and spa?

## Policy & Budget

6. I'm a standard-tier employee. Can I book a business-class flight to Tokyo?
7. What's the hotel budget limit for executive-tier travelers?
8. Is the Marina Bay Sands in Singapore within budget for a standard employee?
9. Can I book with BudgetAir, or do I need to use a preferred vendor?
10. Which preferred airlines fly from SFO to Singapore?

## Events & Seasonal Awareness

11. I'm traveling to Tokyo in early April. Are there any events that could affect pricing?
12. Should I avoid Bangkok during mid-April? Why?
13. How much do Tokyo hotel prices increase during cherry blossom season?
14. Is there anything I should know about traveling to New York in late September?
15. When is the best time to book hotels in London to avoid Wimbledon crowds?

## Availability & Risk Flags

16. Are there any sold-out flights to Bangkok around April 13?
17. The Hilton Tokyo Bay shows 0 rooms available. What are my alternatives?
18. Which Tokyo hotels still have rooms available?
19. The Marriott Sukhumvit Bangkok has no availability. What else is there?
20. Are there any flights to Tokyo with very few seats left?

## Comparison & Trade-offs

21. Compare the Delta non-stop vs United one-stop flight to Tokyo. Which is better value?
22. What's the trade-off between Marriott Shinjuku and Budget Inn Tokyo?
23. For London, should I pick Hilton London City or Marriott Canary Wharf?
24. Is it worth paying more for Singapore Airlines business class to Singapore?
25. Compare all New York hotel options by price and rating.

## Human-in-the-Loop / Verification

26. Book me the most expensive option regardless of policy — will it get flagged?
27. I want to fly first class to Tokyo. What happens with approval?
28. If the system flags my booking for human review, what does that mean?
29. Can you explain why a recommendation might get a low confidence score?
30. What triggers the "suggest with caution" route vs "auto suggest"?
